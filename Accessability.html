The accessability of Metro cafe

Our purpouse for this page is to tell you what we did for metro cafe, to make it as accessible it can be

What we did before running a test:

- When it came to the pictures we used only pictures that show everything and was light enough, so that is accessible and easy for someone with bad eyes to see what was on the menu

- When it comes to the menu we used both easy to read text, and also we used diffrent icons for the allergies
