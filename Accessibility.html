Our effort to make our website accessible for most people

One thing we did to ensure maximum accessibillity for this website, is that we used acceskeys around the site, wich makes it alot easier for users who have difficulty in using pointing devices such as a mouse. We tried to make all our pages as easy to read as possible, with simple navigation, page functions and diffrent icons. Both our text and pictures can be resized without losing any information, and the video content we have has option for subtitles. Theese changes makes it easier for people with both any types of visual impairments, but also people who has any types of auditory impairments
